# The Reinforcement Learning Bibliography: Books & Breakthrough Papers

To master the algorithms in this library, you should refer to these foundational texts and the original research papers that changed the world.

## 📚 Essential Books

### 1. The "Bible" of RL
- **Title**: *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction* (2nd Edition)
- **Authors**: Richard S. Sutton and Andrew G. Barto
- **Why Read?**: This is the absolute foundation. If you want to understand the "Bellman Equation" or "Temporal Difference Learning," this is where you start.
- **Link**: [Free Online Version](http://incompleteideas.net/book/the-book-2nd.html)

### 2. Practical Deep RL
- **Title**: *Deep Reinforcement Learning Hands-On*
- **Author**: Maxim Lapan
- **Why Read?**: Excellent for PyTorch/TensorFlow implementations of modern algorithms like DQN and Rainbow.

### 3. Mathematical Foundations
- **Title**: *Algorithms for Reinforcement Learning*
- **Author**: Csaba Szepesvári
- **Why Read?**: For those who want the rigorous mathematical proofs behind why these algorithms converge.

### 4. Decision Theory
- **Title**: *Decision Making Under Uncertainty*
- **Author**: Mykel J. Kochenderfer
- **Why Read?**: Essential for understanding POMDPs and complex planning in uncertain worlds.

## 📄 Breakthrough Papers (By Category)

### 🚀 The Deep Learning Revolution (Value-Based)
- **DQN (2013/2015)**: *Playing Atari with Deep Reinforcement Learning* (Mnih et al.)
- **Double DQN (2015)**: *Deep Reinforcement Learning with Double Q-learning* (van Hasselt et al.)
- **Rainbow (2017)**: *Rainbow: Combining Improvements in Deep Reinforcement Learning* (Hessel et al.)
- **IQN (2018)**: *Implicit Quantile Networks for Distributional Reinforcement Learning* (Dabney et al.)

### 🎯 Policy Gradients & Actor-Critic
- **TRPO (2015)**: *Trust Region Policy Optimization* (Schulman et al.)
- **DDPG (2015)**: *Continuous control with deep reinforcement learning* (Lillicrap et al.)
- **PPO (2017)**: *Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithms* (Schulman et al.)
- **SAC (2018)**: *Soft Actor-Critic: Off-Policy Maximum Entropy Deep Reinforcement Learning with a Stochastic Actor* (Haarnoja et al.)

### 🔮 Model-Based & World Models
- **MuZero (2019)**: *Mastering Atari, Go, Chess and Shogi by Planning with a Learned Model* (Schrittwieser et al.)
- **Dreamer V3 (2023)**: *Mastering Diverse Domains through World Models* (Hafner et al.)
- **PlaNet (2018)**: *Learning Latent Dynamics for Planning from Pixels* (Hafner et al.)

### 🤝 Multi-Agent RL
- **MADDPG (2017)**: *Multi-Agent Actor-Critic for Mixed Cooperative-Competitive Environments* (Lowe et al.)
- **QMIX (2018)**: *QMIX: Monotonic Value Function Factorisation for Deep Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning* (Rashid et al.)

### 📜 Offline & Imitation RL
- **CQL (2020)**: *Conservative Q-Learning for Offline Reinforcement Learning* (Kumar et al.)
- **GAIL (2016)**: *Generative Adversarial Imitation Learning* (Ho & Ermon)
- **Decision Transformer (2021)**: *Decision Transformer: Reinforcement Learning via Sequence Modeling* (Chen et al.)

### ⚖️ Alignment & Safety
- **RLHF (2022)**: *Training language models to follow instructions with human feedback* (Ouyang et al.)
- **CPO (2017)**: *Constrained Policy Optimization* (Achiam et al.)
